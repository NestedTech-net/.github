# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you identify a potential security issue related to NestedTech systems, report it privately using:

- Contact form: https://nestedtech.net/contact

Please include:
- Affected service/repository (if known)
- Clear reproduction steps
- Expected vs actual behavior
- Potential impact

## Response Expectations
- Initial acknowledgment target: within 3 business days
- Triage target: within 7 business days
- Status updates: provided for accepted reports until resolution

## Coordinated Disclosure
Do not publish exploit details or sensitive data publicly before coordinated disclosure is complete.

## Out of Scope
- Issues requiring unauthorized access attempts against third-party systems
- Social-engineering tests without prior written authorization
- Reports without actionable reproduction detail
